Business Partners


Cambridge has many business partners. Together, we transform ideas into ground-breaking applications and products. These collaborations range from research projects and enterprise initiatives through to recruitment and the provision of executive and professional development courses.

The Research Services Division works with external organisations to develop and maximise research outcomes for long-term mutual benefit. Cambridge Enterprise is responsible for the commercialisation of technology arising from the University’s research. The Development Office raises philanthropic funds in support of our high-profile 800th Anniversary Campaign.
