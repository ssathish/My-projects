Community and Schools


Every year Cambridge staff and students invest 370,000 hours of their time in voluntary and outreach work, with more than 1 million people annually participating in and benefiting from our community activities. Search the Community Directory to find out how you can get involved yourself, or how you can access one of the many resources available.
