International Cambridge


Excellence in teaching and learning is all about people and our international community of staff and scholars bring valuable diversity to our work. Our growing cohort of international students informs and enriches our activities, contributing to our ranking as one of the world's leading universities.

We engage across the globe in virtually every area of research and our work is renowned within the UK and abroad as being internationally excellent.

International Summer Schools
During July and August you have the chance to study and live in one of the world's most renowned universities for between one and six weeks. Our programmes cover a wide range of academic subjects, so whether you are looking to gain extra credit or attain a life-long ambition of studying at Cambridge, spend a very different summer with us.
